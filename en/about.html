About Shiratsuki Hiyori

Personal Introduction & Social Media

Shiratsuki Hiyori · Self Introduction

Shiratsuki Hiyori — a companion-style VTuber who brings gentle comfort amidst moonlight and falling petals.
I hope to bring you healing and happiness by being a companion. Hobbies: Rhythm games, drawing practice, ACG culture, Japanese learning, and chatting with everyone!
Streaming content: Game livestreams (mainly rhythm and indie games), real-time interaction with viewers ~ I won’t let you feel like you’re “just passing by.”
I’ll also try new things from time to time, such as interactive Live2D content!
I love challenging new fields and am working hard to improve my drawing skills and video editing abilities.
If you’re tired or worn out in life, take a break. I’ll be quietly waiting for you in this soft moonlight.
Dream: To become a true “healing-type” VTuber, someone who can gently watch over you when you’re exhausted—not for the sake of popularity, but for a heartfelt connection between us.
Message: Would you like to stop for a moment and rest?
Hiyori will always be here, waiting for you to come back ~
